Hello There!

I am a Student of Pandit Deendayal Energy University formerly known as Pandit Deendayal Petroleum University. Currently pursuing a four-year course in Information Communication and Technology. I enthusiastically lead group projects and mentor peers.

I have a major inclination towards Artificial Intelligence as it amuses me how a machine can be taught to function and cater to our needs and it exactly does the same. I am currently writing a Research Paper on providing a solution in Seismology of drilling oil wells using AI and also have done a project where the machine automatically detects the emotion of the speaker just by listening to the tone of the speakers which you can see in the projects section.

My specialties include quickly learning new skills and programming languages, problem solving, domain driven design, responsive design principles, website optimization. So far I have C++, Java, SQL, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Git/GitHub under my belt. I've started learning React, Node.JS, MongoDB, and Express.JS. I'm still enthusiastically grabbing onto any other programming languages, frameworks, or principles I can integrate into the coding web in my head.

I love applying responsive design principles and watching my web pages shrink into mobile screens and still look amazing. It's oddly satisfying. I think in a way I'm kind of like those web pages; I'm moldable, but I still keep my creative flare intact.
